## History / Social Studies
- [“Major economic developments of 2023 and how they’ll evolve in 2024” (Dec 2023) – Brookings Institution](https://www.brookings.edu/articles/major-economic-developments-of-2023-and-how-theyll-evolve-in-2024/)【12†L132-L139】【14†L178-L182】 – Expert commentary analyzing recent economic trends (evidence-based, cause-and-effect reasoning, opportunities for data interpretation questions).  
- [“Social-behavioral findings can be highly replicable, six-year study by four labs suggests” (Nov 9, 2023) – UCSB News](https://news.ucsb.edu/2023/021257/social-behavioral-findings-can-be-highly-replicable-six-year-study-four-labs-suggests)【16†L78-L83】 – Science news article on a multi-site psychology experiment (focus on study design and replication; supports quantitative evidence and inference questions).  
- [“Is History History? Identity Politics and Teleologies of the Present” (Aug 17, 2022) – AHA Perspectives](https://www.historians.org/perspectives-article/is-history-history-identity-politics-and-teleologies-of-the-present-september-2022/)【41†L69-L77】 – Scholarly essay (by James H. Sweet) on historical interpretation and presentism (rich in rhetorical analysis and evidence-based argument, suitable for evaluating conflicting viewpoints).

## Science
- [“Study finds ChatGPT gets science wrong more often than you think” (Mar 17, 2026) – WSU/ScienceDaily](https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/03/260317064452.htm)【23†L21-L24】 – Research news summarizing an experiment testing AI on scientific hypotheses (multi-phase study design, data on accuracy/inconsistency; ideal for questions on experimental method and inference).  
- [“How thoughtful experimental design can empower biologists in the omics era” (Aug 6, 2025) – *Nature Communications*](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41467-025-62616-x)【25†L39-L47】 – Open-access perspective article on principles of experimental design in biology (discusses replication, controls, randomization; provides high-utility academic vocabulary and rationale for robust research methods).
